üó∫ What is Fortnite?

Fortnite is a massively popular online battle royale game developed by Epic Games. Players from around the world compete by dropping onto an island, gathering resources and weapons, building structures for defense, and battling it out to be the last person or team standing. With vibrant graphics, regular updates, and creative modes, Fortnite has become a cultural phenomenon in the gaming world.

  • Released: 2017
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Switch, Mobile
  • Genres: Battle Royale, Creative Sandbox
  • Players: Solo, Duos, Squads & Special Modes
